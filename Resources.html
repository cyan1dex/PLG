Protection Law Group LLP is a law firm which specializes in all facets of Employment / Labor Law which includes; Class Action Litigation, Donning and Doffing Claims, Expense and Tool Reimbursement Claims, Improper Timekeeping/Recordkeeping Claims, Misclassification Claims (Improper Classification as Exempt and Independent Contractor), Meal Break Claims, Off the Clock Claims (including minimum wages, pre-shift work, training and travel time claims), Overtime Claims, Rest Break Claims, Rounding Hours/Shaving Minutes, Travel Time Claims, Unpaid Bonus and Commission Claims, Unpaid Wages Claims (including piece rate, reporting time and split shifts), Vacation Pay, Medical Leave/Failure to Accommodate Discrimination, Disability Discrimination (Mental and Physical), Family Medical Leave Discrimination, Gender/Transgender/Sexual Orientation Discrimination, National Origin and Race Discrimination, Pregnancy Discrimination, Race, Gender, Disability & Pregnancy Harassment Claims, Religious Discrimination, Retaliation and Whistleblower Claims, Sexual Harassment in the Workplace, and Workers Compensation Claim Discrimination. Our Los Angeles, California law firm is dedicated to giving its clients the very best representation and remains committed to having only the very best attorneys handling their cases.


We provide a wide range of legal services to our clients. We represent California counties including Orange County, Los Angeles County, San Diego County, San Bernardino County and Riverside County.

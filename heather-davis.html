Heather Davis

Ms. Davis handles all areas of employment law and was recognized as one of the top class action attorneys at her former law firm, the largest Labor and Employment law firm in the United States which is solely dedicated to representing employers. She has represented dozens of Fortune 500 corporate companies throughout the country. Her practice includes the representation of companies in state and federal courts in California, Colorado, Texas, Kansas, Minnesota, Tennessee, Missouri, Arkansas and Oklahoma. During her legal career, Ms. Davis has gained the tactical experience in handling employment cases and led teams of lawyers in obtaining successful results in dozens of class actions. Ms. Davis was one of the few attorneys who helped to develop law regarding claims brought under the Private Attorney General Act, Cal. Lab. Code ยง (space) 2699 et seq.

Her representation in the wage and hour realm has included handling complex cases regarding minimum wage violations, overtime wages, meal and rest periods, wage statement violations, donning and doffing, business expenses, misclassifications of managers and independent contractors. Ms. Davis also handles employment lawsuits involving claims of discrimination and harassment based on gender, race, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, pregnancy, age and disabilities.

Her experience includes:

  • Managed wage and hour class actions for numerous large national retailers involving claims of off-the clock work, missed lunch and break periods
  • Successfully litigated class action involving age discrimination involving complex wage and hour claims
  • Defended Fortune 500 employer in cases involving alleged misclassification of independent contractors as employees
  • Handled defense of a national retailer in a wage and hour class action involving claims of misclassified exempt managers
  • Successfully defended multi-plaintiff sexual harassment cases on behalf of employers in both the retail and media industries
  • Strategically managed four separate class actions involving claims against national banking institution for claims of misclassification to obtain a highly favorable resolution
  • Obtained Ninth Circuit affirmation of decision upholding national employee benefit plan on behalf of entertainment company
  • Represented Fortune 5 Company in dozens of cases involving wage and hour and discrimination cases
  • Extensively litigated a class action brought against a security services firm involving meal and rest break claims

Ms. Davis continues to zealously represent employers and employees in her practice and uses her experience working in the largest Labor and Employment law firm in country to ensure that her clients receive not only the skill and resources typically associated with attorneys working in larger law firms but the genuine relationship often established with a client in smaller firms. By using her big firm experience in a small firm setting, she has the keen ability to provide practical advice in the midst of complex and evolving area of law. Ms. Davis' successful representation of employers allows her a unique perspective in representing employees in litigation against employers.

Ms. Davis uses her experience in and out of the courtroom, including her trial experience in litigating individual and class action cases involving wage and hour violations and discrimination. She continues to counsel and advise clients on all facets of employment law, including the negotiation of executive agreements, training for Human Resource Professionals and managers.
